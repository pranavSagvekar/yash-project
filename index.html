Welcome to Explore Koyna

Explore Koyna is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Maharashtra, India. This eco-tourism destination is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, rich biodiversity, and tranquil atmosphere. It's a place where you can reconnect with nature, discover inner peace, and experience the wonders of the Western Ghats.

The Koyna region is home to lush evergreen forests, cascading waterfalls, and a diverse array of flora and fauna. The Koyna River, a lifeline of the region, meanders through the valleys, offering stunning views and opportunities for boating and other water activities.

Things to Do

Explore Koyna offers a wealth of activities. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast, a nature lover, or simply seeking a relaxing getaway, you'll find something to suit your interests.

  • Trekking and Hiking: Explore the numerous trails that wind through the forests and hills, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Water Activities: Enjoy boating, kayaking, and fishing on the Koyna River and nearby lakes.
  • Wildlife Spotting: Keep an eye out for the diverse wildlife that inhabits the region, including birds, deer, and other animals.
  • Visit Waterfalls: Witness the beauty of cascading waterfalls, especially during the monsoon season.
  • Explore Local Culture: Immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting nearby villages, temples, and historical sites.
  • Relax and Rejuvenate: Simply unwind and enjoy the peace and serenity of this natural paradise.

Plan Your Visit

Explore Koyna is a year-round destination, with each season offering a unique experience. The monsoon season (June to September) brings lush greenery and gushing waterfalls, while the post-monsoon and winter months (October to February) offer pleasant weather for outdoor activities. Summer (March to May) is warmer, but still offers opportunities to enjoy the natural beauty of the region.

We offer a variety of accommodations to suit your needs, from comfortable guesthouses to eco-friendly resorts. Our website provides detailed information on how to reach Explore Koyna, the best time to visit, and the various attractions and activities available.
